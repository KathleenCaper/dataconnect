DataConnect is a week-long event organised by the Central Digital and Data Office and the Government Data Quality Hub, this year the event will run from 25 - 29 September 2023. This event is being run by the public sector for the public sector, and we're developing a schedule in collaboration with Local Government, public sector and central government data professionals just like you.

Join us for a variety of virtual and in person sessions on everything from data governance to data architecture and data ethics.
